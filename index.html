Chart 1: Network Visualization of Crypto-Fiat Trading Pairs

This graph shows the relationships between fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies in Binance, highlighting key connections and the centrality of each currency.

Observation: Currencies like EUR and BRL show significant connections with multiple cryptocurrencies, indicating active markets in these regions.

Chart 2: Distribution of Unique Cryptocurrencies by Fiat Currency

This chart illustrates the distribution of unique cryptocurrencies linked to different fiat currencies, emphasizing markets with the highest activity, such as Turkey and the European Union.

Observation: The Turkish Lira (TRY) and Euro (EUR) have the highest number of unique cryptocurrencies, suggesting significant market diversity.

Chart 3: Presence of Top 10 Cryptocurrency-Fiat Pairs

This chart displays the top 10 cryptocurrency-fiat pairs, highlighting the dominance of Bitcoin and USDT in key markets.

Observation: Bitcoin and USDT are heavily favored across all markets, with notable activity in emerging economies.

Chart 4: Evolution of the Crypto-Fiat Trading Network (2021-2024)

This graph shows the evolution of cryptocurrency-fiat trading volumes, identifying the growing complexity in interactions over time.

Observation: USDT's dominance increases significantly by 2024, especially in emerging markets like Turkey.

Chart 5: Monthly Transaction Volumes for Cryptocurrencies

This time-series chart highlights trends in monthly transaction volumes for cryptocurrencies, particularly Bitcoin and USDT, across different regions.

Observation: USDT transaction volumes have surged in recent months, particularly in EMDEs, while Bitcoin shows steadier growth.

Chart 6: Cryptocurrency Trading Volumes by Economic Region

This chart compares cryptocurrency transaction volumes between advanced economies (AEs) and emerging market and developing economies (EMDEs).

Observation: EMDEs prefer USDT for its stability, while AEs continue to favor Bitcoin as a long-term investment.

Chart 7: Annual Cryptoasset Volumes by Region (2024)

This chart illustrates annual cryptocurrency volumes by region, highlighting adoption differences between EMDEs and AEs.

Observation: USDT dominates in EMDEs, driven by macroeconomic instability, while Bitcoin holds a stronger position in AEs.
